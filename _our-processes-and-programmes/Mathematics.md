<h2><strong>Mathematics Curriculum Overview</strong></h2>
<p><em>Building Confident Problem Solvers for the Real World</em>
</p>
<p>At Evergreen Primary School, our Mathematics curriculum is closely aligned
with the <strong>MOE 2021 syllabus</strong> and is designed to nurture <strong>confident, logical, and adaptable problem solvers</strong>.</p>
<p>From Primary 1 to Primary 6, students experience a <strong>progressively structured programme</strong> that:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Strengthens <strong>foundational numeracy</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Builds <strong>fluency in mathematical operations</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Deepens <strong>conceptual understanding</strong> through real-world applications</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>We engage students through a variety of <strong>differentiated learning experiences</strong> and
teaching approaches, including:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Activity-based learning</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Inquiry-based exploration</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Direct instruction</strong>, enhanced with <strong>Information and Communication Technology (ICT)</strong> tools</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Developing 21st Century Competencies</strong></h3>
<p>Our Mathematics programme also supports the development of <strong>21st Century Competencies (21CC)</strong> such
as:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Critical and inventive thinking</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Collaborative problem-solving</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Effective mathematical communication</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Students are encouraged to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Explore multiple strategies</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ask meaningful questions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Reflect on their learning</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Tackle challenges with <strong>confidence and perseverance</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Through these experiences, our students grow into <strong>resilient thinkers</strong> who
can reason logically and solve problems creatively—skills essential for
success in school and beyond.</p>

<h3><strong><br>Key Mathematics Programmes</strong></h3>
<p><em>Inspiring Joy, Building Confidence, and Stretching Potential</em>
</p>
<h4><strong>TGIF: Generating Interest in Mathematics</strong></h4>
<p>The <strong>TGIF Programme</strong> is designed to spark curiosity and enhance
students’ enjoyment of Mathematics by offering meaningful opportunities
to explore the subject beyond the classroom.</p>
<p>In collaboration with the Science Department, TGIF features a series of <strong>hands-on activities</strong> that
integrate both Mathematics and Science concepts. Through <strong>games, puzzles, and interactive tasks</strong>,
students engage in creative exploration, critical thinking, and collaborative
problem-solving in a fun and stimulating environment.</p>
<p>Beyond academic enrichment, TGIF also supports <strong>social-emotional learning (SEL)</strong>.
As students work and play together, they strengthen their communication,
collaboration, and decision-making skills—building confidence and resilience
along the way.</p>
<blockquote>
<p>TGIF is more than just a programme; it’s a joyful, collaborative space
where learning is active, meaningful, and fun.
<br>
</p>
</blockquote>
<hr>
<h4><strong>LSM: Providing Support in Learning Mathematics</strong></h4>
<p>At Evergreen Primary School, we believe that <strong>every child can learn Mathematics</strong> when
given the right support and opportunities.</p>
<p>The <strong>Learning Support for Mathematics (LSM)</strong> Programme is
an early intervention initiative for Primary 1 to Primary 4 students who
need additional help in developing foundational mathematical concepts and
skills. It also aims to nurture <strong>resilience and confidence</strong>,
empowering students to persevere and succeed in their learning journey.</p>
<p>Students in the LSM programme learn in <strong>small groups</strong>, guided
by specially trained teachers who use strategies grounded in the <strong>Singapore Teaching Practice (STP)</strong> and <strong>ICAN principles</strong>.
This supportive environment promotes both academic progress and emotional
growth.</p>
<blockquote>
<p><em>The LSM programme plays a vital role in ensuring that every learner builds a strong mathematical foundation and is given the opportunity to thrive.</em>
</p>
</blockquote>
<hr>
<h3><strong>Stretch Programmes: Empowering Learners to Thrive and Excel</strong></h3>
<p>We recognise that students learn at different paces and have diverse strengths.
Our <strong>tiered stretch programme</strong> nurtures students at various
readiness levels, helping them grow into <strong>confident and competent problem solvers</strong>.</p>
<h4><strong>Tier 1: Building Strong Foundations with Heuristic Skills</strong></h4>
<p>All students are introduced to a variety of <strong>heuristic strategies</strong> throughout
their six years at Evergreen. Using the <strong>STAR approach</strong>—Study
the problem, Think of a plan, Act on it, Reflect on the solution—students
develop strong conceptual understanding and confidence in tackling mathematical
tasks.</p>
<h4><strong>Tier 2: E2K Mathematics – Inquiry and Exploration</strong></h4>
<p>Selected Primary 3 to Primary 5 students participate in the <strong>E2K Mathematics Programme</strong>,
which deepens mathematical reasoning through <strong>inquiry-based learning</strong>.
Students engage in puzzles, games, and open-ended tasks that promote curiosity,
creativity, and collaborative thinking.</p>
<h4><strong>Tier 3: Olympiad Training and Mathematics Competitions</strong></h4>
<p>High-progress learners from Primary 4 to Primary 6 are invited to join <strong>Olympiad training and competitions</strong>,
where they tackle complex, non-routine problems and advanced mathematical
concepts. Through specialised coaching and exposure to competitions, students
sharpen their <strong>higher-order thinking skills</strong> and develop a
positive mindset toward challenging tasks.</p>
<blockquote>
<p><em>Through this structured and progressive approach, we empower every learner to discover their strengths, deepen their curiosity, and stretch their mathematical abilities.</em>
</p>
</blockquote>
