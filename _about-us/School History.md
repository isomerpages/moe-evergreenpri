<p><strong>About Evergreen Primary School</strong>
</p>
<p>Evergreen Primary School was established in January 1999. The school began
operations with 22 classes, catering to pupils from Primary One to Primary
Four.</p>
<p>Initially, it was temporarily housed across two campuses: Campus 1 at
Woodlands Ring Primary School and Campus 2 at Woodlands Ring Secondary
School. On 22 March 1999, the school moved to its current premises. The
new building was officially opened on 6 July 2002.</p>
