<p>The Percussion Ensemble provides students with the opportunity to explore
and learn various percussion instruments, fostering their musical talents
and creativity.</p>
<p>Students in the Percussion Ensemble will learn to play a variety of percussion
instruments, including drums, cymbals, and xylophones. They will develop
rhythm, coordination, and musical expression.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/CCA%20Photos/WhatsApp_Image_2023_09_21_at_15_56_44.jpg">
</div>
<p>Additionally, students will collaborate with peers to create and perform
ensemble pieces. This experience will enhance their listening skills and
musical appreciation.</p>
