<p><strong>Welcome to EGPS Choir!</strong>
</p>
<p>Step into a world where voices come alive, harmonies resonate, and a passion
for music singing blossoms. Our Choir is more than just a group of singers;
it's a supportive family that celebrates the art of choral music and nurtures
every member’s talent.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>A World of Music</u>
</p>
<p>Joining our Choir opens the door to a rich tapestry of musical experiences.
Choir members explore a wide variety of genres, languages, and cultural
traditions, broadening their<s> </s>horizons and fostering an appreciation
for the beauty of choral music.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>Building Confidence and Teamwork</u>
</p>
<p>Participation in the Choir CCA is a journey of personal and collective
growth. Through regular performances, members build confidence while learning
the importance of teamwork as they harmonise their voices to create beautiful
melodies.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>Mastering Choral Skills</u>
</p>
<p>Under the guidance of experienced instructors, students refine their vocal
techniques and develop essential choral skills. This solid foundation helps
them achieve greater heights in their singing journey.</p>
<p></p>
<p><u>Showcasing Talent</u>
</p>
<p>Our Choir members have the opportunity to showcase their talents on various
occasions and platforms<s> </s>such as the school’s annual Achievement
Day and Singapore Youth Festival (SYF) Arts Presentation. These opportunities
allow members to shine and share their music with the broader community.</p>
<p></p>
<p><u>Cultivating Character</u>
</p>
<p>Beyond music, being part of the Choir instills resilience and patience.
Through the challenges of rehearsals and performances, students cultivate
these essential qualities, which will serve them well throughout their
lives.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Join us in EGPS Choir CCA and embark on a fulfilling musical journey that
enriches your school experience and beyond. Together, we create music that
inspires, connects, and unites!</p>
<p></p>
